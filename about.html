From Humble Beginnings

Luis’s Lemonade is inspired by a lemonade stand started by its founders- Luis and Lois Lotikii -12 years ago. The stand was famous for novel lemonade favors like: Vanilla, Chocolate, Batman black cola, and fantastic four fruits. Through Luis’s creative mixes, and Lois’s business skills the stand was a hit. During their time running the stand Luis and Lois created the principles Luis’s Lemonade lives by today: Healing, Community, and Creativity.

Healing

What does your cup of Luis’s ”Turnt Toppled Over DrankXXX” have to do with healing? Everything. Here at a Luis’s Lemonade we believe that eating well made food, that you enjoy can help to improve your health. That’s we use only organic, fair traded products to make our mixes and go out of our way to give all our drinks some medicinal value.

Community

Some of the best memories people have made are those were there with the people they love sharing a meal. In every drink we serve, and at every event we cater we bring a sense of community to everything we do.

Creativity

We here at Luis’s Lemonade take beverage’s to the next level. For us creativity is not adding a drink umbrella, food coloring, and calling it add. We experiment with everything from physical properties of the drink it self… (not done)

The Founders

Luis Lotikii

Luis Lotikii is an award winning mixologist, born, and raised in New York City. He first started mixing when at the age of 12, when he and his older Sister- Lois Lotikii- started a lemonade stand that became famous for its wild lemonade favor designed by Luis. Since then Luis has continued to create new and even more create beverages, and now is bringing his expertise to the public arena.

Lois Lotikii

Lois Lotikii is the business brains behind the Luis’s Lemonade. Her experiences as a graduate of the Wharton school of Business, and an executive of various startups has allowed her to take her little brother’s creative drinks into a thriving business.
